# Pixel Thoughts

**Source**: [pixelthoughts.co](https://www.pixelthoughts.co)
**Creator**: Marc Balaban
**Type**: 60-second meditation tool
**Style**: Conceptual, space-themed

## Overview

Pixel Thoughts is unique - it's not just a breathing exercise, but a concept-driven meditation. You "put a stressful thought in a star" and watch it shrink into the vastness of space. Creative approach to mindfulness.

## Visual Design

### The Star
- **Glowing orb**: White/cream center with warm orange glow
- **Glow effect**: Soft radial gradient, warm colors
- **Size**: Medium-sized, centered on screen
- **Animation**: Shrinks during the meditation

### Background
- **Space theme**: Deep dark blue/black
- **Stars**: Small white dots scattered across viewport
- **Parallax**: Subtle depth with different star sizes
- **Atmosphere**: Creates sense of infinite space

### Colors
- **Background**: Near-black (#0a0a1a)
- **Star center**: Cream/white
- **Glow**: Warm orange (#ff8c42)
- **Stars**: White dots at varying opacities
- **Text**: White, handwritten style font

## Interaction Flow

1. **Landing**: "Pixel Thoughts - A 60-second meditation tool"
2. **Input**: "Put a stressful thought in the star" with text field
3. **Animation**: Star shrinks with your thought
4. **Perspective shift**: "In the vastness of space..."
5. **Completion**: Thought becomes insignificant pixel

## Unique Elements

### Conceptual Framing
- Uses metaphor (thought as star in space)
- Creates meaning beyond just breathing
- Perspective-shifting narrative

### Typography
- Handwritten/script style font
- Personal, intimate feeling
- Contrasts with vastness of space

## Technical Notes

- Canvas or CSS for star field
- Simple but effective
